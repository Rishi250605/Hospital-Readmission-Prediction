About ReadmitAI

ReadmitAI is a demonstration project that predicts the risk of hospital readmission using machine learning and explainable AI (XAI) techniques.

  • Predictsreadmission risk for discharged patients based on clinical and demographic data.
  • Provides transparent explanations for each prediction using SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations).

How does it work?

The model uses a Random Forest classifier trained on anonymized patient data. It considers factors such as age, length of stay, diagnoses, prior visits, lab results, and medication changes.

For each prediction, the system provides a detailed explanation of the most influential features and their impact on the risk score.
